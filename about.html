Rollercoasters are found all over this round ball of a planet we call earth. In order to experience the best of them, you need to make a plan.

A bucket list is a great way to make dreams happen.

Start making your rollercoaster bucket list today!

  1. Decide what rollercoaster experiences you are after. Do you want to ride the fastest, longest, the one with the highest drop, most loops, every single one in your country?
  2. Make a list of the must do rollercoaster rides, according to your rollercoaster experience wish.
  3. Do it! Work through the list and have the time of your life!
  4. Share your story and photos with us.
