Rollercoasters are found all over this round ball of a planet we call earth. In order to experience the best of them, you need to make a plan.

A bucket list is a great way to make dreams happen.

Start making your rollercoaster bucket list today!

    Steps:

  1. Decide what rollercoaster experiences you are after. Do you want to ride the fastest? Or the longest? Or the one with the highest drop or the most loops? Perhaps even tick off every single one in your country?
  2. Make a list of the must do rollercoaster rides, according to your rollercoaster experience wish. We can help you get started with our comprehensive list of rollercoasters.
  3. Do it! Work through the list and have the time of your life!
  4. Share your story and photos with us.
